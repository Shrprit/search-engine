Established under the aegis of Ballaram Hanumandas Charitable Trust, BRCM College of Engineering & Technology started its journey in August 1999.
BRCM-CET represents the manifestation of modern concepts of teaching pedagogy related to higher technical education judiciously blended with the ethos of Indian culture and tradition. The College is duly approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the Government of Haryana and is affiliated to Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak and Chaudhary Bansi Lal University, Bhiwani for 4-year B.Tech and 2-year M.Tech programmes.
